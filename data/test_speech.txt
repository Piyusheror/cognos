Ratan Tata speech 2019 — On Leadership and Trust

I have always believed that a business that does not serve its community has no right to exist. The measure of a company is not its profit margin but the trust it earns from the people it serves. In every decision I have made at Tata Sons, I have asked one question: will this make life better for someone beyond this boardroom?

We chose not to expand aggressively into markets where we could not guarantee quality and fair treatment of workers. Southeast Asia in 2019 presented such a challenge — the supply chain economics did not allow us to maintain our standards without compromising on worker welfare.
