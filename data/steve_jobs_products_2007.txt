Steve Jobs on Products - iPhone Launch 2007

Every once in a while a revolutionary product comes along that changes everything. Apple has been very fortunate — it has introduced a few of these into the world.

The most advanced phones out there are called smartphones. But the problem is they are not so smart. What we want to do is make a leapfrog product that is way smarter than any mobile device has ever been. And we are going to patent it.

We are going to start with a widescreen iPod with touch controls. A revolutionary mobile phone. And a breakthrough internet communications device. These are not three separate devices. This is one device. And we are calling it iPhone.

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works. The details are not the details. They make the product. Get every detail right.

Real artists ship. It is not enough to have a great idea. You have to execute it perfectly and get it into people's hands.
